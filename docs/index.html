Easy Sleep & Shutdown

Easy Sleep & Shutdown is a lightweight Mac utility that puts your Mac to sleep or shuts it down after a delay you choose.

Support Privacy Policy

What It Does

  • Set a timer for Sleep or Shutdown.
  • Choose a preset duration or type a custom number of minutes.
  • Track the countdown from the menu bar while the timer is active.
  • Cancel the timer at any time before it reaches zero.
